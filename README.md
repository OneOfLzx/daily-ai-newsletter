#### 👉 **Read Now**: 🔗 **[oneoflzx.github.io/daily-ai-newsletter](https://oneoflzx.github.io/daily-ai-newsletter/)**

## ✨ What you’re subscribing to

This is a **daily briefing** built for people who want to keep up with artificial intelligence and developer momentum **without living in a hundred feeds**. Each edition brings together fresh stories and notable repositories in one place, written to be **quick to scan** and **easy to share**.

## ⏱️ Why it exists

News and hype move fast. The point here is simple: **save your time**, cut the noise, and still see **what actually showed up today**—in plain language, with links when you want to go deeper.

## 📰 What you’ll find in each issue

Every day you get **short summaries of AI-related news** drawn from a fixed set of trusted pages and RSS feeds, plus **snapshots of trending GitHub repositories** ⭐ so you can see what the community is starring and forking—not just headlines, but **what each repo is about** in a few sentences. Summaries are produced with **AI** 🤖 so the edition stays **consistent and readable** from one day to the next.

## 🗺️ Where the stories come from

We monitor a defined list of websites, blogs, and feeds (and a curated trending-repo source). If you want the exact names and URLs, they’re all listed here: [`config.example.yaml`](config.example.yaml).
